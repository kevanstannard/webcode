--- layout: assignment title: Sign Up Process ---

Your task is so write a simple multi-step sign up process.

There are four steps in the process.

In step 1 collect a username and email address.

In step 2 collect a pricing plan. The selected plan should be highlighted.

In step 3 collect a payment method. The selected payment method should be highlighted.

In step 4 display a summary.

Notes

The Next and Back buttons allow navigation between the pages.

All values and selections should be remembered.

The pages should work on both small and large displays and should adjust their widths according to the size of the container.

Enhancements

Feel free to enhance your application with nicer styles or improved functionality
