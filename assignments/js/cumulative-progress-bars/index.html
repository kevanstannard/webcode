--- layout: assignment title: Cumulative Progress Bars ---

Your task is so write a simple application that shows several progress bars with button controls to increase and decrease their values plus an overall total progress bar.

The + button increases the percentage by 20.

The - button decreases the percentage by 20.

The Total progress bar shows the combined overall progress of all of the progress bars.

Percentages should never go over 100 or below 0.

Notes

The application should be easily configured to show as many progress bars as required.

The application should work on both small and large displays and should adjust it's width according to the size of it's container.

Enhancements

Feel free to enhance your application with nicer styles or improved functionality
